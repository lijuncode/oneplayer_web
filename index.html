    Supports almost all audio formats

    Multiple public formats such as MP3, AAC, WAV, FLAC, AIFF, M4R, m4a, ape, ogg, opus, wma, dsf, dsd, etc., supporting Hi-Res audio quality

      Super convenient song import

      • Wi-Fi import

        Connect your phone and computer to the same Wi-Fi network, and you can easily import songs

      • USB cable import

        If there's no Wi-Fi, you can also import using a USB cable

      • Import from "Files" App

        You can directly import music from the local "Files" App on your phone

      • Import local music from iTunes/iPod music library on your phone

        Supports importing local music from the iTunes/iPod music library on your phone

      • Import via AirDrop

        Supports importing music shared via AirDrop

      Manage your music library

      • Multiple list types

        Supports displaying and managing all your local music by songs, albums, playlists, artists, files, etc.

      • Multiple sorting methods

        Can be sorted by song name, artist name, album name, import time, play count, recently played, etc.

      • Support for editing song information

        Supports editing and modifying song name, artist, album, cover, lyrics, and other song tag information

      • AI cover matching, automatic cover matching
